Right Context Panel — What Shows Here?

This panel sits beside the chat. What information is most useful during study sessions?

1

Performance Score

Overall mastery %, per-topic scores, recent quiz results. Visual progress bars. Shows improvement trend.

2

Weak Topics Alert

Topics where score is below threshold. Spaced repetition due items. "Review needed" badges with days since last practice.

3

Active Study Plan

Current plan progress. Today's tasks checklist. Upcoming milestones. Deadline countdown.

4

Pinned Resources

Recently curated resources, uploaded PDFs, bookmarked links. Quick-access to study materials mentioned in chat.

5

AI Memory / Context

What the AI remembers about you: learning style, past struggles, preferences. Visible so user trusts the personalization. Editable.

6

Study Streak & Stats

Daily streak, total study time, questions answered today. Gamification elements. Contribution calendar.
